About our Business


Inspired by the legacy of a candy shop owner from her childhood, Kristy Vliet, owner of 5 Kidz Kandy, was passioned to replicate the joyous, family-friendly environment that left an impact on her so long ago.


5 Kidz Kandy became the kind of place you can walk into whether you are 2 years old or 50 years old, and you can find something to smile at.


Established in Waynesburg, Pennsylvania in 2015, 5 Kidz Kandy filled the needed niche of a shop where you can run in and grab a gift, ice cream, some chocolates, or something to drink. The store's name was inspired by the combined five children of Kristy and her business partner at the time.


As 5 Kidz Kandy strengthens Waynesburg's community, it continues to grow in itself. The store has expanded to carrying fresh baked goods, dozens of espresso and hand-crafted beverage options, breakfast and lunch, catering, and even custom cookies!


Be sure to stop by and say hi to Kristy and her crew! Their location at 78 E. High Street in Waynesburg is hard to miss!
